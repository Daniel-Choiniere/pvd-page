    PVDFest Returns to the Creative Capital for 2019



    The City of Providence announced that PVDFest, the Citys signature arts festival, will return for a fifth year June 6-9, 2019. As it has in the past, PVDFest will transform downtown Providence into a global celebration of art, music, food, performance and more..!

    City of Providence Searching for a Perfect Tree



    The City of Providence will host the annual holiday tree lighting ceremony on Friday, November 30 at Providence City Hall and Saturday, December 1 at the Alex and Ani City Center Skating Rink in Kennedy Plaza. To complete preparations, the City needs a 35-foot to 45-foot spruce or fir tree to grace the steps at City Hall.
